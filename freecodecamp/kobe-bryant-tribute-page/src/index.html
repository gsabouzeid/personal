Kobe Bryant

The Black Mamba

Here is a timeline of Kobe Bryant's Career:

  • 1996 - Drafted with the 13th pick by the Charlotte Hornets
  • 1997 - Won the Slam Dunk Contest
  • 1998 - Played his first all star game, one of many
  • 2000 - Won his first championship against the Indiana Pacers
  • 2006 - Scored 81 points in a single game against the Toronto Raptors
  • 2008 - Won his first and only MVP award averaging 28.3 ppg, 5.4 apg, and 6.3 rpg
  • 2010 - Won his last and fifth championship against the rivaled Boston Celtics
  • 2014 - Surpassed his basketball idol, Michael Jordan, in total points scored
  • 2016 - Scored 60 points in his final NBA game before he retired
